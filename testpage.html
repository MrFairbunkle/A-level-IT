GREAT CORNARD INFO

Operating from 1987 until 1999, the Mid Night Club was one of the most notorious and highly respected street racing clubs to ever grace the Wangan highway between Tokyo and Yokohama.

The Mid Night Club was established with a strict code of ethics to stop any of its members from putting the general public or other club members at risk. The Mid Night Club used to race along the Wangan motorway between Tokyo and Yokohama at sustained racing speeds of up to 300 km/h (190 mph), hence entry requirements for Mid Night were strict. The minimum requirements were that you drove a car capable of exceeding 250 km/h (160 mph).
